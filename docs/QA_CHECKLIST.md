# QA Checklist Before Sending Work to Client

## Website
- No spelling or grammar mistakes.
- Mobile layout checked.
- Phone number is clickable.
- Appointment button is visible above the fold.
- Contact form tested.
- No copied content from another dental clinic.
- Page titles and meta descriptions added.
- Analytics/Search Console installed.
- Privacy policy page exists.

## Dental compliance
- No guaranteed ranking claims.
- No guaranteed patient/revenue claims.
- No fake reviews or paid review language.
- Before/after photos are clinic-approved.
- Treatment claims are clinic-approved.
- Province-specific advertising rules are respected.

## White-label
- No subcontractor logo.
- No subcontractor email or phone.
- No developer portfolio link.
- Client-facing documents use agency brand.
- Internal costs hidden from client users.

## SEO
- Service pages have unique copy.
- City and province targeting is natural, not spammed.
- GBP categories/services are accurate.
- NAP details are consistent.
- Image alt text is relevant.
